Navajo silversmith Gary Reeves makes great pieces of traditional silver. This is one of the oldest techniques used by the Navajo, sandcasting. This cuff is an old design that shows that wonderful character of sandcast silver. Gary decorates the silver with a row of blue spider web China Mountain Turquoise stones. You will find a total of five stones across the top, all with that classic blue and black look. When you wear this traditional sandcast bracelet you will definitely be showing your appreciation of Navajo handmade art.
